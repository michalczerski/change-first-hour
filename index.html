First hour
for God

Give the first hour of your day to God.
Simple. Every day.

This is the most important hour of the day—the first one in the morning. How you spend this hour shapes the rest of your day.

If you rush and your thoughts are scattered, your day will reflect that.

How to spend this hour?

1
Wake up at the same time. Don't stay "just 5 more minutes" in bed.
2
Listen to your thoughts. Notice those about hurry, worries, and fears—don't feed them.
3
Give them to God. Ask God to take these thoughts. Clear your mind for Him.
4
Bring your cross to Jesus. Ask Him to help with heavy emotions and states.
5
Track your progress. Write down what happens in your heart each day.
6
Give thanks before you act. Remember to thank the Lord before you begin.
7
Keep your mind clear. Don't let bad thoughts take root. Go out every day, no matter the weather—show your determination and seriousness on this path.

Simple prayer

"Lord, take away my fear and worries.
I open my mind and heart to You.
Lead me in this hour and throughout the day. Amen."

Let God lead you in this transformation.
